Conditionality

What must be done to receive the transfer? Conditional transfers are contingent upon the beneficiary fulfilling prerequisite conditions in order to receive a transfer. This includes cash for work. Unconditional transfers are delivered without prerequisite conditions.

Restriction

What can the transfer be used for? Restricted transfers (often delivered as a voucher) can only be used on a particular type of good/ service or redeemed at a particular vendor. Unrestricted transfers can be spent however the recipient chooses.
